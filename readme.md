# World And System Proceduraliser Sim (WASPS)
- by YF

In recent decades, NASA launched the WASP (Wide Angle Search for Planets) program, which contributed to the discovery of over 200 exoplanets. Of course, we cannot visit these planets in person anytime soon (in fact, I doubt we will ever be able to in our lifetimes), but that doesn't mean we can't explore then *through a simulation*. 

Building on the philosophy of discovery of WASP, WASPS is a procedural, algorithmic space simulation that you use to generate and explore your own, randomly generated, *planetary systems* in `three.js`. I hope that, where our current spaceflight technology is limited, WASPS can provide an alternative (albeit virtual) way to explore the universe and satisfy, at least in part, our pursuit of understanding exo-systems.
